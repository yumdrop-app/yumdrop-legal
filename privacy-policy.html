YumDrop Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

YumDrop is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Name (optional)
  • Email address
  • User ID
  • Photos you upload for recipes
  • Recipes you save
  • App usage data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide YumDrop services
  • To generate AI-powered recipes
  • To sync your data across devices
  • We never sell your data to third parties

4. Data Storage

All data is stored securely with Google Firebase in the European Union (europe-west1) with industry-standard encryption.

5. Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app (Settings → Profile → Delete Account) or by submitting a request at: https://forms.gle/J93dRJXWB8mdXBgaA

6. Contact

For privacy questions: privacy@yumdrop.app
